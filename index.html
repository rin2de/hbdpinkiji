HAPPY BIRTHDAY SHONA


On this day, my dear wife, I want to say,
That I'm grateful for you in every way.
Your love and kindness, your gentle touch,
Make my life complete, and mean so much.

Today is your birthday, and I want you to know,
How much I love you, and how much I owe,
To you, for all the joy and happiness you bring,
To my life, every day, like the sunshine in spring.

You are my soulmate, my partner, my friend,
With you by my side, I know we'll never bend.
Through thick and thin, in every storm we'll weather,
Together, forever, we'll face whatever.

So let's celebrate your special day,
With joy and laughter, in every way.
May your heart be filled with love and cheer,
And may all your dreams come true this year.

Happy birthday, my love, my dear wife,
You are the light that brightens my life.
May this birthday bring you all you desire,
And fill your heart with love, forever and entire.
